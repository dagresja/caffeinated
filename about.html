About us


We started Caffeinated Inc. back in 1998 as a small business project born out of pure love and passion for perfectly-brewed coffee. We wanted it to be a place where everyone feels welcome, whether they wish to work, relax or simply spend some time with friends. We took advanced barista course, rented a place, bought the best coffee machine available at that time, decorated the interiors with what limited budget we had and... opened our doors to our first guests. Although we started as a 3-member team with just one coffee maker, we soon realized we would have to triple or even quadruple our resources in response to the increasingly high demand for our services. Now, with almost 30 years of meticulous coffee-making experience, we offer top-quality Italian coffee, a wide selection of finest tea of all colours and flavours, filling yet healthy lunch options and - of course - the sweetest and most delicious of desserts.
